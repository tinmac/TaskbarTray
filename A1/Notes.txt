Instlling the Service
=====================
Installing from PowerScript

Set-ExecutionPolicy Bypass -Scope Process -Force

Get-Service -Name SensorService_Labs
Start-Service -Name SensorService_Labs
Stop-Service -Name SensorService_Labs
sc.exe delete "SensorService_Labs"



sc.exe config SensorService_Labs obj= LocalSystem
Restart-Service -Name SensorService_Labs



Install manually in PS 
- Open PS as Admin in the folde where install-service.ps1 is located
- type: .\install-service.ps1
